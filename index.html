About

Biochem Master

A lightweight offline study companion for undergraduate biochemistry. Study data and progress remain on this device unless you export them.

  • Built with HTML5, CSS3, vanilla JavaScript, and JSON.
  • No account, backend server, database, or external API is required.
  • Designed for offline studying on desktop and iPad.

Biochem Master stores study progress locally on this device.
